## Important Notes

1. **Verify claims independently** - Many institutes claim "100% placement guarantee." Always ask for verifiable alumni testimonials and placement data before enrolling.

2. **Check accreditations** - Look for EC-Council, CompTIA, NASSCOM, IIFIS, or government accreditations to ensure course quality.

3. **Visit campus** - For offline courses, always visit the campus and attend a free demo class before making payment.

4. **Placement vs. Assistance** - "Placement guarantee" and "placement assistance" are fundamentally different. Clarify this distinction before enrolling.

5. **Fee verification** - Fees marked "est." or "approx." are based on market research data. Always confirm current pricing directly with the institute.

6. **EMI options** - Most institutes offer EMI/installment payment options. Ask about no-cost EMI availability.

7. **Certification costs** - Some fees may not include exam/certification voucher costs (e.g., CEH exam voucher is separate ~INR 25,000-30,000).
